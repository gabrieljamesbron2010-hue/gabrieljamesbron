Siargao

Siargao is a tear-drop shaped tropical island in the Philippines, globally renowned as the country's "Surfing Capital". Located in Surigao del Norte, it features world-class surf breaks like Cloud 9, crystal-clear lagoons, white-sand beaches, and lush palm forests, making it a premier destination for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

EnchantedRiver

The Hinatuan Enchanted River in Surigao del Sur, Philippines, is a deep, saltwater spring known for its surreal, crystal-clear blue waters. Flowing into the Pacific Ocean, its exact source remains unexplored. Surrounded by lush greenery, it gets its magical name from local folklore about fairies and mysterious, uncatchable fish.
